Frequently Asked Questions

Is AI Video Editor really free?

Yes! The application is completely free and open source. You'll need your own OpenAI API key for AI features, which operates on a pay-as-you-go basis.

What video formats are supported?

We support all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and more. The app uses FFmpeg for video processing, ensuring broad compatibility.

Do I need an OpenAI API key?

Yes, for AI-powered features like transcription and content analysis, you'll need an OpenAI API key. You can get one from OpenAI's platform at platform.openai.com.

How long does processing take?

Processing time varies based on video length and your computer's specs. Typically, a 10-minute video takes 2-5 minutes to process into multiple short clips.

Can I customize the generated clips?

Absolutely! You can set preferences for clip duration, content type (high energy, insights, emotional peaks), maximum number of clips to generate, and adjust silence removal thresholds.

How does the silence removal work?

Our intelligent jumpcutter analyzes audio levels and automatically removes silent sections, pauses, and quiet parts. This keeps your content engaging without manual editing, saving you hours of work.

Is my data kept private?

Your videos are processed locally on your computer. Only transcription and analysis data is sent to OpenAI's API. We don't store or track your content.
